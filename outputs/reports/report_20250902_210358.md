# Review Report

### 창 (Window)

*   **Item:** 창 G1
*   **Field:** 창호열관류율 (Window U-value)
*   **User Input:** 2.4 W/m²K
*   **Correct Value:** 1.300 W/m²K
*   **Reason for Correction:** The input value of 2.4 W/m²K does not meet the legal energy performance standard for the building's location. For the '대구_경산' region, the '건축물의 에너지절약설계기준' requires that the U-value for windows in direct contact with outside air must not exceed 1.300 W/m²K [1].
*   **Required Change:** The '창호열관류율' for G1 must be changed to **1.300**.

*   **Item:** 창 G2, G3
*   **Field:** 일사에너지투과율(-) (SHGC)
*   **User Input:** 0.471
*   **Correct Value:** 0.474
*   **Reason for Correction:** The design drawing specifies the glass for these windows as '사중창' (quadruple glazing) with a soft Low-E coating and a 12mm air gap. According to the standard values provided in the '건축물의 에너지절약설계기준', the correct SHGC for this specific glass assembly is 0.474 [2]. The user's input of 0.471 does not match this standard value.
*   **Required Change:** The '일사에너지투과율' for both G2 and G3 must be changed to **0.474**.

### 문 (Door)

*   **Item:** 문 D1, D2
*   **Field:** 창호열관류율 (Window U-value)
*   **User Input:** 3.8 W/m²K
*   **Correct Value:** 1.500 W/m²K
*   **Reason for Correction:** The input value of 3.8 W/m²K does not meet the legal energy performance standard for the building's location. For the '대구_경산' region, the '건축물의 에너지절약설계기준' requires that the U-value for doors in direct contact with outside air must not exceed 1.500 W/m²K [1].
*   **Required Change:** The '창호열관류율' for both D1 and D2 must be changed to **1.500**.
