# Review Report

## 기계설비부문_난방기기 (Mechanical Equipment_Heating Equipment)

### Item: YH-1
The equipment 'YH-1' must be removed from the model.
- **Reason for Correction**: The purpose of this equipment is listed as '화장실 동파방지' (frost prevention in the restroom) in the equipment schedule [4]. According to the review guidelines, equipment used for frost prevention is not subject to evaluation and should not be included in the model [2].
- **Correction**: Delete the 'YH-1' entry from the heating equipment list.

### Item: B-1
The '기기효율(%)' (Equipment Efficiency) for this item is incorrect.
- **Reason for Correction**: The value entered in the model is **70%**. However, the efficiency must be calculated based on the specifications in the equipment schedule [3].
    - Rated Heating Capacity: 14,500 kcal/h
    - Fuel Consumption: 1.97 L/h
    - Fuel Calorific Value: 8,300 kcal/L
- **Correction**: Based on the calculation formula from the manual (Efficiency = Rated Heating Capacity / (Fuel Calorific Value × Fuel Consumption)) [2], the correct efficiency is **88.7%**. The value for the '기기효율(%)' field must be changed to **88.7**.

### Item: EHPS - 14
The '성적계수(COP)' (Coefficient of Performance) for this item is incorrect.
- **Reason for Correction**: The value entered in the model is **2**. However, the COP must be calculated based on the specifications in the equipment schedule [1].
    - Rated Heating Capacity: 36,980 kcal/h (43.0 kW)
    - Power Consumption (Heating): 13,000 W (13.0 kW)
- **Correction**: Based on the calculation formula from the manual (COP = Rated Heating Capacity / Power Consumption) [2], the correct COP is **3.31**. The value for the '성적계수(COP)' field must be changed to **3.31**.
