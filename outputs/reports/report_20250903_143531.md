# Review Report

### 건축부문_외피_형별성능내역 (Building Part_Envelope Performance by Type)

*   **Item**: Floor Type `F2`
    *   **Field**: `바닥난방여부` (Floor Heating Status)
    *   **Issue**: The floor heating status is entered as `false`, but the drawings indicate the presence of radiant floor heating.
    *   **Model Input**: `false`
    *   **Evidence**: The `Equipment_Oil_Boiler_2F_Heating_Piping_Plan.png` drawing shows radiant floor heating piping installed in the area corresponding to floor type `F2` [1].
    *   **Correction**: For the envelope type `F2`, the `바닥난방여부` field must be changed to `true`.

### 건축부문_외피_형별성능내역_재료 (Building Part_Materials for Envelope Performance by Type)

*   **Item**: Wall Type `W1`
    *   **Field**: `두께(mm)` (Thickness) for `비드법보온판1종1호`
    *   **Issue**: The thickness of the insulation material is entered incorrectly.
    *   **Model Input**: `1500` mm
    *   **Evidence**: The `Architecture_Envelope_Composition_Materials_Walls_Floor_Roof.png` drawing specifies a thickness of `150` mm for `비드법보온판 1종1호` in the `W1` assembly [2].
    *   **Correction**: In the material list for `W1`, the `두께(mm)` of `비드법보온판1종1호` must be corrected from `1500` to `150`.

### 건축부문_외피 (Building Part_Envelope)

*   **Item**: Wall Type `W2`
    *   **Field**: `방위` (Orientation) and `면적(m²)` (Area)
    *   **Issue**: The orientation for all `W2` wall sections is incorrectly set to `일사없음` (no solar radiation). The areas are also split into multiple entries that should be consolidated by their correct orientation.
    *   **Model Input**: Five separate entries with `방위` set to `일사없음`, with a total area of 75.33 m².
    *   **Evidence**: The `Architecture_Envelope_Insulation_Area_Calculation_1F.png` drawing shows that `W2` walls are exposed to the South, East, and West [3]. The areas are:
        *   South: `38.61` m²
        *   East: `12.96` m²
        *   West: `23.76` m²
    *   **Correction**: Delete the five existing entries for `W2`. Create three new entries for `W2` with the correct orientation and consolidated area for each:
        *   One entry with `방위` as `남` and `면적` as `38.61` m².
        *   One entry with `방위` as `동` and `면적` as `12.96` m².
        *   One entry with `방위` as `서` and `면적` as `23.76` m².

*   **Item**: Floor Type `F1`
    *   **Field**: `면적(m²)` (Area)
    *   **Issue**: The area for the lowest floor is entered incorrectly.
    *   **Model Input**: `224.91` m² for the entry described as `바닥(최하층)`.
    *   **Evidence**: The `Architecture_Envelope_Insulation_Area_Calculation_1F.png` drawing specifies a total area of `216` m² for `F1` [3].
    *   **Correction**: For the `F1` entry, the `면적` must be corrected from `224.91` to `216`.

*   **Item**: Floor Type `F2`
    *   **Field**: `면적(m²)` (Area)
    *   **Issue**: The area for the floor below the pit is entered incorrectly.
    *   **Model Input**: `26.73` m² for the entry described as `바닥(피트하부)`.
    *   **Evidence**: The `Architecture_Envelope_Insulation_Area_Calculation_1F.png` drawing specifies a total area of `35.64` m² for `F2` [3].
    *   **Correction**: For the `F2` entry, the `면적` must be corrected from `26.73` to `35.64`.

*   **Item**: Roof Type `R1`
    *   **Field**: `면적(m²)` (Area)
    *   **Issue**: The area for the 2nd-floor roof is entered incorrectly.
    *   **Model Input**: `74.58` m² for the entry described as `지붕(2층)`.
    *   **Evidence**: The `Architecture_Envelope_Insulation_Area_Calculation_2F.png` drawing specifies an area of `46.86` m² for the 2nd-floor roof [4].
    *   **Correction**: For the `R1` entry described as `지붕(2층)`, the `면적` must be corrected from `74.58` to `46.86`.
