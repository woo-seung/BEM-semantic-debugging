# Review Report

### Wall Assembly Material Thickness

In the `건축부문_외피_형별성능내역_재료` section, the thickness of a material in the **W1** wall assembly is incorrect.

- **Field to Correct**: `두께(mm)`
- **Material**: `비드법보온판1종1호`
- **Incorrect Model Value**: 1500 mm
- **Correct Value from Drawings**: 150 mm [2]

The thickness for this material should be changed from 1500 mm to **150 mm**.

### Wall Orientations

In the `건축부문_외피` section, several 1st-floor walls of type **W2** have been assigned an incorrect orientation. The `방위` for these walls is entered as `일사없음`, but they should be assigned to the orientations specified in the design drawings.

- **Field to Correct**: `방위`
- The following entries, currently set to `일사없음`, must be corrected:
    - The wall with an area of **38.61 m²** should have its orientation changed to **`남`** (South). [3]
    - The wall with an area of **9.72 m²** should have its orientation changed to **`동`** (East). [3]
    - The wall with an area of **3.24 m²** should have its orientation changed to **`동`** (East). [3]
    - The wall with an area of **19.44 m²** should have its orientation changed to **`서`** (West). [3]
    - The wall with an area of **4.32 m²** should have its orientation changed to **`서`** (West). [3]
