# Review Report

### 건축부문_외피_형별성능내역 (Architectural Part_Envelope_Performance by Type)

*   **Item:** `G2`, `G3`
*   **Field:** `일사에너지투과율` (SHGC)
*   **Issue:** The entered Solar Heat Gain Coefficient (SHGC) is incorrect.
*   **Description:**
    *   **User Input:** `0.471`
    *   **Required Value:** `0.474`
*   **Correction:** The architectural drawings indicate that window types `G2` and `G3` have a glass composition of '사중창, 22mm[5(CL)+12(Air)+5(LE,소프트코팅)] 2겹' [1]. As no separate performance test report was submitted, the SHGC value must be based on the standards provided in the '건축물의 에너지절약설계기준' [2]. According to this standard, the correct SHGC for this glass composition is `0.474`. Please change the `일사에너지투과율` for items `G2` and `G3` to `0.474`.
