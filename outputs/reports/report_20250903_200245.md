# Review Report
## 기계설비부문_난방기기 (Mechanical Equipment_Heating Equipment)

### Electric Boiler `YH-1`
*   **Issue**: The equipment `YH-1` is included in the evaluation, but it should be excluded.
*   **Reason**: The equipment schedule indicates that the purpose of `YH-1` is '화장실 동파방지' (anti-freeze for restroom) [2]. Equipment used for anti-freeze purposes is not subject to evaluation.
*   **Correction**: Please remove the `YH-1` equipment from the model.

### Heat Pump `EHPS - 14`
*   **Field**: `성적계수(COP)` (Coefficient of Performance)
*   **Issue**: The input value for the Coefficient of Performance (COP) is incorrect.
*   **Details**:
    *   **Model Input**: `2`
    *   **Correct Value**: `3.31`
*   **Reason**: The COP is calculated by dividing the rated heating capacity by the power consumption. Based on the equipment schedule, the rated heating capacity is 43 kW (36,980 kcal/h) and the power consumption is 13.0 kW [1]. The calculated COP is therefore 43 kW / 13.0 kW = 3.31.
*   **Correction**: Please change the `성적계수(COP)` for `EHPS - 14` from `2` to `3.31`.

### Boiler `B-1`
*   **Field**: `기기용량` (Equipment Capacity)
*   **Issue**: The input value for the equipment capacity is incorrect.
*   **Details**:
    *   **Model Input**: `16.83` kW
    *   **Correct Value**: `16.86` kW
*   **Reason**: The equipment schedule lists the rated heating capacity as 14,500 kcal/h [2]. Converting this to kW (14,500 kcal/h ÷ 860 kcal/h/kW) results in 16.86 kW.
*   **Correction**: Please change the `기기용량` for `B-1` from `16.83` to `16.86`.

*   **Field**: `기기효율` (Equipment Efficiency)
*   **Issue**: The input value for the equipment efficiency is incorrect.
*   **Details**:
    *   **Model Input**: `70` %
    *   **Correct Value**: `88.68` %
*   **Reason**: The efficiency is calculated by dividing the rated heating capacity by the energy input from the fuel. Based on the equipment schedule, the rated capacity is 14,500 kcal/h, the fuel consumption is 1.97 L/h, and the fuel's calorific value is 8,300 kcal/L [2]. The calculated efficiency is 14,500 kcal/h / (1.97 L/h × 8,300 kcal/L) = 88.68%.
*   **Correction**: Please change the `기기효율` for `B-1` from `70` to `88.68`.

## 기계설비부문_냉방기기 (Mechanical Equipment_Cooling Equipment)

### Heat Pump `EHPS - 14`
*   **Field**: `열성능비(COP)` (Thermal Performance Ratio)
*   **Issue**: The input value for the Coefficient of Performance (COP) is incorrect.
*   **Details**:
    *   **Model Input**: `2`
    *   **Correct Value**: `3.01`
*   **Reason**: The COP is calculated by dividing the rated cooling capacity by the power consumption. Based on the equipment schedule, the rated cooling capacity is 40.6 kW (34,916 kcal/h) and the power consumption is 13.5 kW [1]. The calculated COP is 40.6 kW / 13.5 kW = 3.01.
*   **Correction**: Please change the `열성능비(COP)` for `EHPS - 14` from `2` to `3.01`.
