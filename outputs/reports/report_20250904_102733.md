# Review Report

## 건축부문 외피_형별성능내역_재료 (Building Envelope > Type Performance Details > Materials)

### Wall Component W1
*   **Issue:** The thickness of the insulation material is incorrect. This appears to be a unit error (mm instead of m, or an extra zero).
*   **Field:** `두께(mm)`
*   **Material:** `비드법보온판1종1호`
*   **Current Input:** 1500 mm
*   **Correct Value:** 150 mm [1]
*   **Correction:** In the material composition for the wall type `W1`, change the thickness of the `비드법보온판1종1호` from 1500 mm to 150 mm.

## 건축부문 외피 (Building Envelope > Envelope)

### Wall Component W2
*   **Issue:** The orientation for all `W2` wall components is incorrectly set to `일사없음` (No Solar Radiation). As `W2` is defined as an external wall in direct contact with the outside air, it should have a cardinal orientation to account for solar gains.
*   **Field:** `방위`
*   **Current Input:** `일사없음` for all `W2` wall entries on the 1st floor.
*   **Correct Value:** The orientations should be `남`, `동`, and `서` based on the insulation area calculation table [2].
*   **Correction:** Modify the `방위` for the following 1st-floor `W2` wall entries:
    *   Change the orientation of the wall with an area of 38.61 m² to `남`.
    *   Change the orientation of the walls with areas of 3.24 m² and 9.72 m² to `동`.
    *   Change the orientation of the walls with areas of 4.32 m² and 19.44 m² to `서`.

### Floor Components F1 and F2
*   **Issue:** The areas for the floor components `F1` and `F2` do not match the values in the design documents.
*   **Field:** `면적(m²)`
*   **Current Input:**
    *   `F1` (`바닥(최하층)`): 224.91 m²
    *   `F2` (`바닥(피트하부)`): 26.73 m²
*   **Correct Value:**
    *   `F1`: 216.00 m² [2]
    *   `F2`: 35.64 m² [2]
*   **Correction:**
    *   For the `바닥(최하층)` entry that uses component `F1`, change the `면적(m²)` from 224.91 to 216.00.
    *   For the `바닥(피트하부)` entry that uses component `F2`, change the `면적(m²)` from 26.73 to 35.64.
