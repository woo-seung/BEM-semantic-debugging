# Review Report

### 기계설비부문_난방기기 (Mechanical Equipment_Heating Equipment)

*   **Device: YH-1**
    *   **Issue:** The device 'YH-1' (전기라디에이터) should not be included in the model.
    *   **Reason:** The equipment schedule indicates its purpose is '화장실 동파방지' (preventing freezing in toilets). According to the modeling guidelines, equipment for anti-freeze purposes is not subject to evaluation and must be excluded from the model [1].
    *   **Correction:** Please delete the 'YH-1' device from the heating equipment list.

*   **Device: B-1**
    *   **Field:** 기기효율(%) (Equipment Efficiency (%))
    *   **Issue:** The equipment efficiency is entered incorrectly.
    *   **Model Value:** 70%
    *   **Correct Value:** 88.68%
    *   **Reason:** Based on the equipment schedule, the Rated Heating Capacity is 14,500 kcal/h, fuel consumption is 1.97 L/h, and the fuel's calorific value is 8,300 kcal/L. The efficiency calculated from these values is 88.68% [2].
    *   **Correction:** Please change the '기기효율(%)' for device 'B-1' from 70 to 88.68.

*   **Device: EHPS - 14**
    *   **Field:** 성적계수(COP) (Coefficient of Performance (COP))
    *   **Issue:** The heating COP is entered incorrectly.
    *   **Model Value:** 2
    *   **Correct Value:** 3.31
    *   **Reason:** Based on the equipment schedule, the Rated Heating Capacity is 36,980 kcal/h (43.0 kW) and the power consumption is 13.0 kW. The COP calculated from these values is 3.31 [2].
    *   **Correction:** Please change the '성적계수(COP)' for device 'EHPS - 14' from 2 to 3.31.

### 기계설비부문_냉방기기 (Mechanical Equipment_Cooling Equipment)

*   **Device: EHPS - 14**
    *   **Field:** 열성능비(COP) (Coefficient of Performance (COP))
