# Review Report

### Windows G2, G3

*   **Section**: 외피_형별성능내역
*   **Field**: 일사에너지투과율(-)
*   **Issue**: The Solar Heat Gain Coefficient (SHGC) value is incorrect.
*   **Details**:
    *   **Model Input**: 0.471
    *   **Evidence-Based Value**: 0.474
*   **Reason for Correction**: The drawings indicate that windows G2 and G3 are '사중창, 22mm[5(CL)+12(Air)+5(LE,소프트코팅)] 2겹' (quadruple-glazed units) [1]. According to the '건축물의 에너지절약설계기준' (Building Energy Conservation Design Standards), the standard SHGC for a quadruple-glazed unit with a low-emissivity coating and a 12mm air gap is 0.474 [2].
*   **Required Action**: For items **G2** and **G3**, change the `일사에너지투과율` value from **0.471** to **0.474**.
