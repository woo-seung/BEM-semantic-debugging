# Review Report

### 기계설비부문_난방기기 (Mechanical Equipment Section_Heating Equipment)

#### Item: YH-1
*   **Issue**: The equipment `YH-1` (전기라디에이터) has been incorrectly included in the evaluation.
*   **Reason for Correction**: The equipment schedule indicates that the purpose of this device is '화장실 동파방지' (anti-freeze for the restroom) [3]. Equipment used for anti-freeze purposes is not subject to evaluation and should not be included in the model.
*   **Correction**: Please remove the `YH-1` equipment from the heating equipment list.

#### Item: B-1
*   **Field**: `기기효율(%)` (Equipment Efficiency (%))
*   **Issue**: The efficiency of the boiler is entered incorrectly.
*   **Details**:
    *   **Model Input**: 70%
    *   **Evidence-Based Value**: The equipment schedule for `B-1` (기름보일러) shows a rated heating capacity of 14,500 kcal/h, fuel consumption of 1.97 L/h, and a fuel calorific value of 8,300 kcal/L [2]. Based on the calculation method (Efficiency = Rated Heating Capacity / (Fuel Consumption × Fuel Calorific Value)), the correct efficiency is 88.68% (14,500 / (1.97 × 8,300)).
*   **Correction**: Change the `기기효율(%)` for `B-1` from 70 to **88.68**.

#### Item: EHPS - 14
*   **Field**: `성적계수(COP)` (Coefficient of Performance (COP))
*   **Issue**: The heating COP of the heat pump is entered incorrectly.
*   **Details**:
    *   **Model Input**: 2
    *   **Evidence-Based Value**: The equipment schedule for `EHPS - 14` shows a rated heating capacity of 36,980 kcal/h (which is 43.0 kW) and a rated power consumption of 13.0 kW [1]. Based on the calculation method (COP = Rated Heating Capacity / Rated Power Consumption), the correct COP is 3.31 (43.0 kW / 13.0 kW).
*   **Correction**: Change the `성적계수(COP)` for `EHPS - 14` from 2 to **3.31**.

### 기계설비부문_냉방기기 (Mechanical Equipment Section_Cooling Equipment)

#### Item: EHPS - 14
*   **Field**: `열성능비(COP)` (Energy Efficiency Ratio (COP))
*   **Issue**: The cooling COP of the heat pump is entered incorrectly.
*   **Details**:
    *   **Model Input**: 2
    *   **Evidence-Based Value**: The equipment schedule for `EHPS - 14` shows a rated cooling capacity of 34,916 kcal/h (which is 40.6 kW) and a rated power consumption of 13.5 kW during cooling operation [1]. Based on the calculation method (COP = Rated Cooling Capacity / Rated Power Consumption), the correct COP is 3.01 (40.6 kW / 13.5 kW).
*   **Correction**: Change the `열성능비(COP)` for `EHPS - 14` from 2 to **3.01**.
