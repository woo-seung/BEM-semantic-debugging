# Review Report

### Wall Assembly (벽체)

*   **Item**: Wall Performance Type W1
*   **Location**: 건축부문_외피_형별성능내역_재료 (Building Section_Envelope_Type Performance Details_Materials)
*   **Issue**: The thickness of the insulation material is incorrect.
*   **Description**: For the performance type `W1`, the thickness of the material `비드법보온판1종1호` was entered as 1500 mm. However, the construction drawings indicate a thickness of 150 mm.
*   **Correction**: In the material list for performance type `W1`, change the `두께(mm)` of `비드법보온판1종1호` from 1500 to **150**.

*   **Item**: Wall Performance Type W2
*   **Location**: 건축부문_외피 (Building Section_Envelope)
*   **Issue**: The orientation of the walls is incorrect.
*   **Description**: Multiple wall sections using performance type `W2` were assigned the orientation `일사없음` (No Solar Radiation). According to the area calculation drawings, these walls are external and face South, East, and West.
*   **Correction**: The `방위` (Orientation) for the following `W2` wall entries must be updated:
    *   Change the orientation for the wall with `면적(m²)` 38.61 from `일사없음` to **남**.
    *   Change the orientation for the wall with `면적(m²)` 3.24 from `일사없음` to **동**.
    *   Change the orientation for the wall with `면적(m²)` 9.72 from `일사없음` to **동**.
    *   Change the orientation for the wall with `면적(m²)` 4.32 from `일사없음` to **서**.
    *   Change the orientation for the wall with `면적(m²)` 19.44 from `일사없음` to **서**.

### Floor Assembly (바닥)

*   **Item**: Floor Areas
*   **Location**: 건축부문_외피 (Building Section_Envelope)
*   **Issue**: The areas for floor types F1 and F2 are incorrect.
*   **Description**: The model assigns an area of 224.91 m² to floor type `F1` (`바닥(최하층)`) and 26.73 m² to floor type `F2` (`바닥(피트하부)`). The area calculation drawings specify an area of 216 m² for `F1` and 35.64 m² for `F2`.
*   **Correction**:
    *   For the entry with `형별성능내역` `F1`, change the `면적(m²)` from 224.91 to **216**.
    *   For the entry with `형별성능내역` `F2`, change the `면적(m²)` from 26.73 to **35.64**.
