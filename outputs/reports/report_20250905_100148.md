# Review Report

## 기계설비부문_조명기기 (Mechanical Equipment Sector_Lighting Fixtures)

The lighting fixture information entered in the model is different from the design drawings. All existing entries in the `조명기기` section must be deleted and replaced with the corrected values below.

### 1. 조명기기 종류 (Lighting Fixture Type)
*   **Issue:** The model lists the `조명기기종류` as `형광등` (Fluorescent) and `일반등` (General).
*   **Evidence:** According to the `조명기구일람표` (Lighting Fixture Schedule), all lighting fixtures specified for the building are `LED`. [1]
*   **Correction:** All lighting fixtures entered in the model must have their `조명기기종류` set to `LED`.

### 2. 조명전력(W) 및 대수 (Lighting Power (W) and Quantity)
*   **Issue:** The power ratings and quantities for the lighting fixtures entered in the model do not match the design documents. For example, the model includes 31 units of 10W fixtures and 48 units of 6W fixtures, which are not present in the drawings.
*   **Evidence:** The `조명기구일람표` (Lighting Fixture Schedule) and `전등설비평면도` (Lighting Plan Drawing) specify the exact power rating and quantity for each fixture type. [1]
*   **Correction:** Delete all current lighting fixture entries. Based on the design drawings, add the following five groups of lighting fixtures to the model.

| 조명기기종류 (Lighting Fixture Type) | 조명전력(W) (Lighting Power (W)) | 대수 (Quantity) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| LED | 32 | 31 |
| LED | 13 | 50 |
| LED | 20 | 7 |
| LED | 36 | 4 |
| LED | 60 | 3 |
