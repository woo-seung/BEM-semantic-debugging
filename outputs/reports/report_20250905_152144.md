# Review Report

## 기계설비부문_난방기기 (Mechanical Equipment_Heating Equipment)

### EHPS - 14
*   **성적계수(COP)**: The input value is **2**, but this is incorrect. Based on the design documents, the equipment's rated heating capacity is 43.01 kW and its power consumption is 13.0 kW. According to the review guidelines, the COP is calculated as capacity divided by power consumption.
    *   **Correction**: The `성적계수(COP)` must be changed to **3.31** (43.01 kW / 13.0 kW).

### B-1
*   **기기효율(%)**: The input value is **70%**, but this is incorrect. The review guidelines require calculating the efficiency based on the equipment's specifications. The design documents show a rated heating capacity of 14,500 kcal/h, fuel consumption of 1.97 L/h, and a fuel calorific value of 8,300 kcal/L.
    *   **Correction**: The `기기효율(%)` must be changed to **88.68%** (14,500 kcal/h / (1.97 L/h × 8,300 kcal/L)).

### YH-1
*   **장비 입력 오류**: This equipment is entered as an electric boiler for heating, but the design documents indicate it is an electric radiator (`전기 라디에이터`) used for 'Prevention of toilet pipe freezing' (`화장실 동파방지`) [1]. Equipment used for anti-freeze purposes is not subject to evaluation and should not be included in the model.
    *   **Correction**: The entire entry for **YH-1** must be deleted from the heating equipment list.

## 기계설비부문_냉방기기 (Mechanical Equipment_Cooling Equipment)

### EHPS - 14
*   **장비 중복 입력 및 열성능비(COP) 오류**: The model incorrectly contains two identical entries for this single piece of equipment. Additionally, the `열성능비(COP)` for both entries is **2**, which is incorrect. The design documents show a rated cooling capacity of 34,916 kcal/Hr (40.61 kW) and a power consumption of 13.5 kW.
    *   **Correction**:
        1.  Delete one of the two identical entries for **EHPS - 14**.
        2.  For the remaining entry, change the `열성능비(COP)` to **3.01** (40.61 kW / 13.5 kW).
